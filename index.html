DANCING JAZZ SQUARES

Find the Artist

The jazz square is a dance move with four steps. Those steps form a…you guessed it: SQUARE! Think stepping cross, back, side, front with your feet one at a time. With that in mind, you’ll have 4 tries (*cough, cough* steps) to win the game and find the artist, Bruno. Let’s dance!

Game Rules

  • Click the boxes to find Bruno
  • If you click on the saxophone, you will lose
  • If you click on the painting, you will be a step closer
  • If you click on Bruno, you win
